Front End Engineer

We are looking for a front end engineer with experience using RESTful services to build robust web applications. Ideally this person will have an advanced understanding of MV* patterns and frameworks and have an almost unhealthy preoccupation with well crafted, thoughtfully structured code. An awareness of and appreciation for the field's best practices is required. An interest in defining and contributing best practices back to the community is appreciated.

Absolute base requirements for junior php dev...

  • Javascript, JavaScript, and javascript
  • A well developed understanding of MV* patterns and frameworks
  • Enthusiasm for talking about the intersection of javascript and MV*
  • The ability to work out of our office in NYC

Extra credit if the junior dev...

  • Actually has the initials JS
  • Enjoys Backbone.js
  • Enjoys engaging in philosophical discussion
  • Knows when it's time to stop that discussion and write code
  • Is familiar with or interested in the peculiarities of wine culture

What we look for...

We look for thinking developers. We look for thinking, smiling, developers. We look for thinking, smiling developers who enjoy drinking wine. We look for thinking, smiling developers who enjoy drinking wine and thinking about how we can use code to manipulate the data surrounding that wine in novel and useful ways.

What we offer...

Aside from your run of the mill benefits package we offer developers the space to grow, experiment, learn, influence, and innovate. We provide a unique situation that combines the creative potential of an early stage start-up with the security and sway of a decades old, niche dominant publishing company. We also offer free wine and your choice of gear.
