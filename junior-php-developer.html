Junior PHP Developer

We are looking for a junior php developer to join a team that is focused on building the services that will give our editors, developers, partners, and users the tools they need to create and benefit from a more collaborative and distributed publishing organization. The junior developer will spend his or her time writing the code (php) that will form the foundation of this world class publishing platform. While this is an entry level spot our junior dev can write well structured, object-oriented php and should be familiar with the principles of RESTful web services (you don't need to be Roy Fielding, but it would be great if you know who he is). The JD should also be comfortable developing and documenting APIs for both internal and external developer consumption. Curiosity, eagerness to learn, and the ability to understand and transform complex concepts into clean object-oriented code are extremely important.

Absolute base requirements for junior php dev...

  • PHP fluency, of course
  • The ability to take direction
  • The ability to write reusable php
  • The ability to work out of our office in NYC

Extra credit if the junior dev...

  • Has experience using a php framework (+1 for Kohana)
  • Enjoys engaging in philosophical discussion
  • Knows when it's time to stop that discussion and write code
  • Is familiar with or interested in the peculiarities of wine culture

What we look for...

We look for thinking developers. We look for thinking, smiling, developers. We look for thinking, smiling developers who enjoy drinking wine. We look for thinking, smiling developers who enjoy drinking wine and thinking about how we can use code to manipulate the data surrounding that wine in novel and useful ways.

What we offer...

Aside from your run of the mill benefits package we offer developers the space to grow, experiment, learn, influence, and innovate. We provide a unique situation that combines the creative potential of an early stage start-up with the security and sway of a decades old, niche dominant publishing company. We also offer free wine and your choice of gear.
